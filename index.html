2022 -
I founded The BioML Society, a student-led research group, with my colleague Aaron Feller in Spring 2023. As of Oct 2024 we have 18 completed or ongoing projects with various faculty.

In Fall 2023 I designed and co-taught a lecture series on machine learning for biochemical applciations. This series went on to become an official course taught by Dr. Claus Wilke in Fall 2024.

In Summer 2024 I organized a worldwide protein design competition with Aaron Feller, Daryl Barth, and Phillip Woolley. This competition was covered in Nature and is currently ongoing.
2022 -
My PhD is focused on using AI for small molecule discovery and protein design. I am co-advised by Andrew Ellington and Edward Marcotte.
2020 - 2022
I was a research associate at Beyond Meat where I worked on projects in analytical chemistry (GCMS, lipids), lab automation (liquid handlers, data processing, LIMS), and flavor chemistry.
2016 - 2020
BS in Chemical & Biological Engineering at the University of Colorado. Research on Organic Synthesis in Wei Zhang's lab and microfluidic simulations in Robert Davis' lab.
